About

I'm a Senior Machine Learning Engineer at NVIDIA, focused on AI safety for large multimodal foundation models. I design systems that make powerful AI more reliable, robust, and aligned — working across text, image, audio, and video modalities.

Previously at Electronic Arts and Actium Health, I built ML platforms and infrastructure that scaled data science across organizations — from training job orchestration to model lifecycle management and experiment tracking.

Outside of work, I'm an active AI builder. I like small, useful tools: coding agents, browser extensions, and local-first interfaces that turn messy workflows into something you can actually ship.

I hold a Master's from Carnegie Mellon University and a dual degree in Statistics and Mathematics from the University of Missouri. I care deeply about building AI that is both powerful and responsible.
